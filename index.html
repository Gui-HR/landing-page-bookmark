A Simple Bookmark Manager

A clean and simple interface to organize your favourite websites. Open a new browser tab and see your sites load instantly. Try if for free.

Features

Our aim is to make it quick and easy for you to acess your favourite websites. Your bookmarks sync between your devices so you can acess them on the go.

Bookmark in one click

Organize your bookmarks however you like. Our simple drag-and-drop interface gives you complete control over how you manage your favourites sites.

Intelligent search

Our powerful search feature will help you find saved sites in no time at all. No need to trawl through all of your boomarks.

Share your bookmarks

Easily share your bookmarks and collections with others. Create shareable link that you can send at the click of a button.
